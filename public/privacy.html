🌿 Amahoro Challenge Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

The short version: Your conversations with Mahoro are private and never shared with third parties. We collect only what we need to make Amahoro work for you.

Who we are

Amahoro Challenge is a product of KUZOO.AI, registered in Rwanda (TIN: 156296596). We are based in Kigali, Rwanda. You can reach us at hello@amahoro.app.

What we collect

  • Account information — your email address and name when you sign up
  • Profile information — your country, main worry, and goal (shared during onboarding)
  • Challenge reflections — what you write in daily challenge responses
  • Conversations with Mahoro — your messages and Mahoro's responses
  • Progress data — which challenge days you have completed

What we do not collect

  • We do not collect your location beyond the country you choose
  • We do not collect payment card details — payments are handled by Paystack
  • We do not sell your data to anyone, ever
  • We do not use your data for advertising
  • We do not share your conversations with third parties

How we use your data

  • To personalise Mahoro's responses to your specific situation and country
  • To save your challenge progress so you never lose your journey
  • To load your conversation history when you return
  • To show you aggregate analytics in your progress dashboard

AI and your conversations

Your conversations with Mahoro are processed by Anthropic's Claude AI. Anthropic has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data sent to their API. We do not store your conversations in a way that identifies you to any third party. Your conversations are yours.

Data security

Your data is stored in Supabase, a secure database platform. All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS. Row Level Security means only you can access your own data — not other users, and not our team except for aggregate anonymised analytics.

Your rights

  • You can request a copy of your data at any time — email hello@amahoro.app
  • You can request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • You can update your profile information at any time within the app

Children

Amahoro is designed for youth aged 16 and above. If you are under 16, please use Amahoro with the knowledge of a parent or guardian.

Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes to this policy, we will notify users by email. The date at the top of this page shows when it was last updated.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email us at hello@amahoro.app. We respond within 48 hours.
